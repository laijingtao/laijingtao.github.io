University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign

Hi there!

My name is Jingtao Lai (来景涛). I’m currently a PhD student in Department of Geology at UIUC working with Dr. Alison Anders. My research interests center around the interactions between climate, tectonics, and surface processes and their impacts on landscape evolution. My approach thus far has been to develop numerical landscape evolution models to explore the sensitivity of landscape characteristics to variability in climate, surface process regime, and tectonics.

Interests

  • Numerical landscape evolution model
  • Coupling of climate, tectonics and surface processes
